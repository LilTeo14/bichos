Presentation
What is it?

SEEBUG is an in-situ intelligent entomological monitoring station with a decoupled modular design. It features a Universal Upper Module (high-res camera, cellular transmission, solar power) and interchangeable lower traps. It captures images and transmits them wirelessly to the cloud in real-time, where advanced AI models classify invasive insects.

The Problem

We identified a critical need for an intelligent monitoring network in the agricultural-forestry interface of central and southern Chile. By detecting key invasive insects early, we aim to prevent irreversible damage to the forest canopy before devastating outbreaks occur.

Context & Solution

Context: Arises from the critical need for in situ monitoring in highly vulnerable zones where agricultural lands converge with native forests.

Where: Silvo-agricultural interface of Central and Southern Chile, protecting native ecosystems.

When: 24/7 active remote monitoring to ensure early detection of invasive pests before irreversible damage.

How it works: SEEBUG functions as an autonomous station that lures insects using specific attractants, captures high-res images, and utilizes Cloud AI to classify them in real-time.
